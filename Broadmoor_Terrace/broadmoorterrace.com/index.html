Welcome to Broadmoor Terrace Apartments located in Colorado Springs on South 8th street, close to downtown, major universities, and in a very good school district, with nice views of the city, Cheyenne Mountain and Pikes Peak. We offer a variety of various size apartments ranging from 436 up to 1568 sq ft. Flexible lease options, 6 and 12 month available.

The apartments, built on a 5 acre lot, not only provides a park-like environment where local wildlife often visits, but it gives also ample space for parking. Some tenants have a nice view of the mountains while the others may enjoy spectacular sunrise or sunsets (see photo below - full moon rising over the airport). The Clubhouse has been remodeled, providing room for social events, morning coffee, news and free Wi-Fi. The outdoor pool is heated, enjoy swimming and sun bathing from approximately May thru September. There are new laundry machines in each building. New carpet were just installed in every building and hallway, with ongoing remodeling daily.

Just across the street you'll find a small shopping center with amenities like a fitness club, Dollar General, Library 21, Chinese food, Liquor Store, Cheyenne Mountain Library and additional laundry facilities. Wal Mart is approximately 1.5 miles away to the North. The local bus stop is conveniently located at the edge of the property to the East.
